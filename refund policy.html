Hispano Suiza Refund Policy

At Hispano Suiza, we are committed to providing our customers with exceptional vehicles and service. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please review our refund policy below.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Refunds are available for vehicles purchased directly from Hispano Suiza or authorized dealers.
  • Requests for refunds must be made within 14 days of delivery.
  • The vehicle must be returned in its original condition, without any modifications or damage.
  • All original documentation, accessories, and keys must be included with the return.
  • Proof of purchase is required for all refund requests.
  • Vehicles with excessive mileage may be subject to additional inspection before approval.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Customized or special-order vehicles are not eligible for refunds.
  • Registration fees, taxes, and delivery charges are non-refundable.
  • Vehicles purchased through third-party resellers are not covered by this policy.
  • Any damage incurred after delivery will void refund eligibility.

Refund Process

  • Contact Hispano Suiza customer service or your authorized dealer to initiate a refund request.
  • Once your request is approved, you will receive instructions for returning the vehicle.
  • Vehicles must be returned to the original point of sale unless otherwise arranged.
  • After inspection and approval, refunds will be processed within 14 business days to your original payment method.
  • If your refund is delayed, please contact our support team for assistance.

Exceptions

  • Refunds may be denied if the vehicle shows signs of misuse or unauthorized repairs.
  • Partial refunds may be offered in certain circumstances, subject to management approval.

Contact Us

For questions or to initiate a refund, please contact our customer service team at support@hispanosuiza.com or call +34 123 456 789.

Additional Information

  • Refund policy applies only to purchases made within Spain and select international locations.
  • For international purchases, additional terms may apply. Please consult your dealer.

This policy is subject to change without prior notice. Please refer to our website for the latest information.
